Product Details

Unleash your inner hero with , a bold and unforgettable fragrance inspired by Greek mythology and the power of passion. Named after the god of love, Eros embodies strength, desire, and confidence—crafted for the modern man who lives life on his own terms.
Aromatic Fougère
Scent Type: Fresh, Woody, Oriental
  • Top: Mint Oil, Green Apple, Italian Lemon
  • Middle: Tonka Bean, Geranium Flower

  • Base: Venezuelan Ambroxan, Vanilla, Cedarwood, Vetiver, Oakmoss


Versace Eros opens with an energetic burst of mint oil, crisp green apple, and zesty Italian lemon—delivering a fresh, vibrant start that awakens the senses. The heart introduces the warm complexity of tonka bean and aromatic geranium, lending a smooth, sensual character. The dry-down reveals a masculine base of ambroxan, vanilla, and rich woods, creating a long-lasting and powerful trail that evokes both elegance and primal energy.

The striking turquoise bottle is etched with the iconic Medusa emblem, a symbol of power, seduction, and timeless masculinity. With gold accents and classic Greek key detailing, the flacon is a bold tribute to Versace’s heritage and mythological inspiration.

Created by Aurelien Guichard of Givaudan and launched in 2012, Versace Eros is more than just a scent—it’s a statement. Perfect for day or night, it’s ideal for the man who’s passionate, fearless, and unapologetically himself.
